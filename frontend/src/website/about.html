About Hippo Exchange

Hippo Exchange is your reliable platform to borrow and loan items within your community. We aim to connect people, promote sharing, and reduce waste by giving everyday items a new home.

Whether you need a power drill for a weekend project or want to lend out your camping gear, Hippo Exchange makes it easy and secure.
