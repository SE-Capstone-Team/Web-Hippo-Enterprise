About Hippo Exchange

Hippo Exchange is a peer-to-peer platform where users can easily borrow and loan items. Our mission is to make everyday resources more accessible, reduce waste, and foster community sharing.

Whether you’re lending tools, borrowing equipment, or finding temporary items, Hippo Exchange connects people with the things they need, when they need them.
