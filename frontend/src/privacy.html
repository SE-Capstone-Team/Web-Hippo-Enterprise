Hippo Exchange — Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect
- Personal Information: Name, email, phone, address, payment details.
- Rental Information: Items rented, history, reviews.
- Technical Information: IP, browser, device, cookies.

2. How We Use Information
We use data to:
- Provide and improve rental services.
- Process payments and prevent fraud.
- Communicate updates, promotions, and support.
- Comply with legal obligations.

3. Sharing of Information
We may share data with:
- Payment processors.
- Service providers (hosting, analytics, support).
- Legal authorities when required.
We do NOT sell personal data.

4. Cookies & Tracking
Cookies improve user experience and security. You can manage cookies via browser settings.

5. User Rights
You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data. You can opt out of marketing emails.

6. Data Security
We use protective measures, but no system is 100% secure. Users will be notified of any breaches.

7. Children’s Privacy
Not for children under 13 (or 16 where applicable).

8. International Users
Services are available only within the U.S.

9. Policy Changes
We may update this Privacy Policy and post the new effective date.

10. Contact
For privacy concerns, contact HippoExchange support.
